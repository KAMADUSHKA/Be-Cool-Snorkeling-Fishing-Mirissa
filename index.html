About

At Mirissa Turtle Snorkeling & Fishing Center, we offer unforgettable ocean adventures, including snorkeling, scuba diving, and fishing tours. Dive into the beauty of marine life and create lifelong memories with us.

  • Who We Are?

    Mirissa Turtle Snorkeling & Fishing Center is your gateway to unforgettable marine adventures. We specialize in snorkeling with turtles, scuba diving, thrilling fishing tours, and deep-sea explorations.

  • Safty First

    Your safety is our top priority. At Mirissa Turtle Snorkeling & Fishing Center, we provide high-quality equipment, experienced guides, and thorough safety briefings for all our activities. Enjoy peace of mind as you explore the ocean with us.

  • Expert Guidance

    Our team of experienced and certified guides ensures a safe, enjoyable, and informative experience. Whether you're snorkeling, diving, or fishing, you'll receive expert tips and personalized support every step of the way.

  • Reasonable Prices

    Enjoy unforgettable ocean adventures at competitive prices. We offer exceptional value with flexible packages to suit every budget, ensuring your experience is both affordable and memorable.
